The following table showcases the number of foreign workers in Singapore and the type of work passes they carry. While mid-skilled workers and professionals hold an S pass or Employment Pass (EP), low-skilled migrant workers hold a work permit. Such systemic structures act as obstacles to prevent these migrant workers from settling in Singapore despite their contributions to the nation's economy.
